Based on the given research paper "The Influence of Cost Drivers on Effort Estimation in Distributed Software Development," here is a comprehensive summary of the paper:

**Summary:**

The paper explores the concept of effort estimation in the context of Global Software Development (GSD), focusing on the influential cost drivers that affect effort estimation. The authors aim to provide insights to enhance productivity and minimize costs associated with effort estimation in GSD environments.

**Methodology:**

The authors employ a systematic literature review (SLR) methodology to investigate various aspects of effort estimation in GSD. They utilized six electronic databases, namely IEEE Xplore, ACM Digital Library, Wiley Online Library, Google Scholar, ScienceDirect, and Microsoft Academic. The search query consisted of four terms: "Factors" or "variables", "Effort Estimation", and either "Global Software Development" or "Global Software Engineering". The search yielded 492 articles, from which 35 relevant papers were shortlisted.
